Although we have struggled a lot throughout the years we end up in a pretty good condition. I am bilingual and i'm working on being trilingual. I am fluent in English and Spanish and I know a little bit of French. I have two brothers. The youngest is a Freshman at Arsenal Tech High School and the oldest one is a Sophomore also at Arsenal Tech. I am the class of 2019 at Arsenal Tech. I will be graduating this year with the Academic Honors Diploma. I am very involved at my school and I do many other actvities after school. You could basically call me a busybody.
